Reformers Unanimous

RU Recovery Program

RU (Reformers Unanimous) is a faith-based addiction recovery program that addresses the root causes of addiction through biblical principles. At Landmark Baptist, we believe true and lasting freedom comes through the power of Jesus Christ — not willpower alone.

Our RU program is open to anyone struggling with addiction, compulsive behavior, or life-controlling problems — and to their family members who need support. There is no charge to attend. All are welcome.

K–12 Christian Education

Landmark Baptist School

Landmark Baptist School exists to provide a superior, Christ-centered education in a safe and nurturing environment. We partner with Christian families to train students spiritually, academically, and socially — preparing them for life, college, and eternity.

Our dedicated faculty integrate biblical truth into every subject, fostering academic achievement and a love for God's Word. Small class sizes allow for personalized attention and genuine community.

  • Kindergarten through 12th Grade
  • A.C.E. / ABEKA Christ-centered curriculum
  • Dedicated Christian faculty
  • Located on the LBC campus — Grand Junction, CO 81501
  • Partnering with Christian families

More Ways to Serve

All Our Ministries

Bus Ministry

Our bus routes bring families from across Grand Junction to worship every Sunday — removing barriers to church attendance for those who need a ride.

Music Ministry

Our music ministry leads the congregation in God-honoring worship through the choir, orchestra, and special music — glorifying God with excellence.

Media Ministry

Our media team handles livestreaming, audio, and video production — extending the ministry of Landmark Baptist Church far beyond our four walls.

Soul Winning

Landmark is committed to personal evangelism. Our soul-winning teams go into the community every week to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ door to door.

Ladies' Ministry

Fellowship, Bible study, and service opportunities for women of all ages — including Ladies' Tea events and annual retreats that build lasting friendships.

Men's Ministry

Men's Prayer Breakfast and accountability groups challenge men to lead their families and workplaces with biblical integrity and courage.

Discipleship Program

A 20-lesson one-on-one mentoring program (Room 206) for new believers and growing Christians. Free to anyone who wants to grow in their walk with God.
