Recovery Ministry

RU Recovery
Program

RU (Reformers Unanimous) Recovery Program is a Bible-based, Christ-centered recovery program designed to rescue, recover, and restore those in addictive behaviors with the power of the victorious life found only in Jesus Christ.

Through consistent Bible teaching, accountability, and a caring family atmosphere, individuals are equipped with the spiritual tools they need to walk in lasting freedom. No matter where you've been, there is hope.

"And ye shall know the truth, and the truth shall make you free."

— John 8:32 KJV

K–12 Christian Education

Landmark
Baptist School

Landmark Baptist School is dedicated to providing an exceptional, Christ-centered education for students in kindergarten through 12th grade. Our LBS Eagles are equipped with the academic rigor, Biblical worldview, and character they need to excel — in school and in life.

We partner with Christian families to train up the next generation in the way they should go, anchoring every subject in the unchanging truth of God's Word.

  • Kindergarten through 12th Grade
  • Bible-based curriculum and worldview
  • LBS Eagles sports programs and activities
  • Dedicated, experienced Christian faculty
  • Partnering with Christian families

More Ways to Serve & Connect

All Ministries

Every ministry at Landmark exists for one reason: to glorify God by loving people and reaching our world with the Gospel.

Bus Ministry

Our church buses reach people across the Grand Valley who might not otherwise have a way to attend. Transportation to and from church services — because no one should miss out due to a lack of a ride.

Sunday Services

Music Ministry

Joyful, God-honoring music that elevates worship and touches the heart. From the choir to special music, every song is offered to the glory of God. Opportunities to serve in music are available for those who love to worship through song.

Every Service

Media Ministry

Our media team oversees graphic design, web design, video production, and our sound room — ensuring the message of the Gospel reaches people both inside our walls and across the internet through our YouTube livestream.

Online & On-site

Discipleship Program

A 20-lesson course giving new believers a rock-solid Biblical foundation. The first four weeks meet Sunday mornings in Room 206 at 10:00 AM, followed by one-on-one mentoring through Wednesday night Bible study.
