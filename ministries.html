Faith-Based Recovery

RU Recovery Program

RU (Reformers Unanimous) is a faith-based addiction recovery program that helps individuals overcome life-controlling habits through the power of God's Word. RU addresses the root causes of addiction — not just the symptoms — with a proven, biblically-grounded curriculum.

Whether you are struggling with substance abuse, pornography, gambling, anger, or any other stronghold, there is freedom available through Jesus Christ. You don't have to fight alone.

"I can do all things through Christ which strengtheneth me."

— Philippians 4:13 (KJV)

K–12 Christian Education

Landmark Baptist School

Equipping students with academic excellence and spiritual maturity since our founding.

Grades Served Kindergarten through 12th Grade
Curriculum A.C.E. / ABEKA — Christ-centered academics
Location On the Landmark Baptist Church campus — Grand Junction, CO
Admissions Contact us for enrollment information

Landmark Baptist School exists to provide a superior, Christ-centered education in a safe and nurturing environment. We partner with parents to train students spiritually, academically, and socially — preparing them for life, college, and eternity.

Our dedicated faculty integrate biblical truth into every subject, fostering both academic achievement and a love for God's Word. Small class sizes allow for personalized attention and a genuine sense of community.
