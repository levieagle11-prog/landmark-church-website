Birth Through 6th Grade

Children's Ministry

Starting with excellent nurseries, there is something special for every age child.

Our nurseries (Birth up to 3 years) are safe, clean, and highly staffed by carefully selected and trained mothers. For elementary students (Grades 1–6), our primary and junior classes are filled with games, excitement, crafts, and Bible-centered learning to make church memorable for your child.

🍼

Nursery

Birth – 2 Years

Our baby nursery is properly staffed with loving workers in a clean, safe, and secure location. Each nursery is led by a carefully selected and trained mother. Mothers receive a pager so attendants can easily contact you if there is ever a need.

  • Secure check-in number system
  • Available at every service
  • All volunteers are church members
🎨

Toddlers & Preschool

2 – 3 Years

Your child will come home singing new songs, quoting memorized verses, and displaying crafts. Teachers are trained, dedicated, and excited to be a part of the life of your child as they learn who Jesus is and that God made them for a unique purpose.

  • Songs, crafts & Bible stories
  • Age-tailored curriculum
  • Compassionate, qualified teachers
📚

Elementary

Grades 1 – 6

Games, excitement, learning, crafts, and songs describe our Primary and Junior Sunday School classes. Boys and girls are split into separate classes — a prime environment for making good, lasting friendships. All attend vibrant Junior Church at 11:00 AM.

  • Sunday School at 10:00 AM
  • Junior Church at 11:00 AM
  • Gender-separated classes

Not sure where to go? Our greeters and ushers are stationed across campus and will gladly escort your family to the right rooms. You'll never have to navigate alone.

7th – 12th Grade

Teen Ministry

A strong foundation and a community they'll love coming back to.

Led by Youth Directors Spencer and Tashina Jones, our goal is to give your teen a strong, Christ-centered foundation combined with the friendly atmosphere they enjoy. The teenage years are when we develop a lot of who we are — and we take that seriously.

During all of our activities, your teen will always receive a Bible challenge to help them grow in their young Christian life. We also offer regular parent meetings throughout the year, providing practical instruction on raising teens in this challenging culture.

Spencer & Tashina Jones Youth Directors sjones@lbcgj.com 970-978-1686
