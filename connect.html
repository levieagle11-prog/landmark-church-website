Ages Birth–12

Children's Ministry

Every child who walks through our doors is known, loved, and valued. Our safe, age-appropriate programs build a biblical foundation that lasts a lifetime — all while making Sunday the best day of the week.
👶

Birth – 2 Years

Nursery

A clean, secure, and loving environment for your youngest. Our trained nursery team cares for babies and toddlers during every service so you can worship with peace of mind.

  • Background-checked workers
  • Secure check-in system
🌱

3 – 5 Years

Little Landmarks

Bible stories, worship songs, crafts, and play — our preschool class introduces children to God's Word through hands-on, interactive learning that kids love.

  • Bible memory curriculum
  • Character-building activities
📖

Grades K – 6

Elementary Kids

Our elementary program teaches kids to understand, love, and apply Scripture. Games, lessons, small groups, and special events make faith real and relevant for growing minds.

  • Expository Bible lessons
  • Scripture memorization awards

Your child's safety is our top priority. All workers are background-checked, trained in child safety protocols, and passionate about kids. Our secure check-in system ensures only authorized adults can pick up your child.

Grades 7–12

Teen Ministry

High school and middle school can be the hardest years — and the most formative. Our teen ministry is a place where students find real community, real answers, and a real faith to carry them through life.

Led by Pastor Spencer Jones, our teens dive deep into Scripture, build lasting friendships, and serve others through outreach and missions.
